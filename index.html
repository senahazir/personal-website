Paper Trails is a blockchain forum which enables journalists in difficult locations to convey media, news, and information in a secure manner to news agencies. As a Web3 platform that is aimed to be used for fetching, viewing, and licensing journalistic content stored on a decentralized, persistent file-store blockchain, it enables reporters to publish, view, and track activities in sensitive locations without fear of identity breaches and safety issues.
